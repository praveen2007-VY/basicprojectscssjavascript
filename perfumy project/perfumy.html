About As

At Essenza, we believe fragrance is more than scent—it's memory, mood, and magic in a bottle. Born from a passion for artistry and olfactory elegance, our perfumes are crafted to evoke emotion and express individuality. Each blend is a journey—whether it’s the whisper of jasmine at dusk or the bold embrace of oud at midnight.Rooted in tradition and inspired by modern sophistication, we source the finest ingredients from around the world to create timeless fragrances for every soul. Our mission is simple: to help you wear your story, one spritz at a time.
